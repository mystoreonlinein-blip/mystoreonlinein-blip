Overview

How it works?

Here is how it works for your customers.

  1. User scans QR Code

    Your scans the QR code. The customer could be at home or could be scanning QR visible at store. This opens the store website or mobile application depending the subscription.

  2. Add items to cart

    They add items, share contact and address details, and submit the order.

  3. Customer checkout

    The customer sees a cart that has all the items added.

  4. Order processing

    Depending on how the store owner configured the store, the order would be visible for processing or approval.

  5. Pickup or delivery

    Customers pick up in‑store or receive delivery as per your settings. We have option for live tracking.
