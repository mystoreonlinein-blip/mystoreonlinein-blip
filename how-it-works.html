Overview

How it works?

Here is how it works for your customers.

  1. User scans QR Code

    Your customer walks in, scans the QR code.

  2. Add items and place order

    They add items, share contact and address details, and submit the order.

  3. You confirm and prepare

    You receive the order instantly. Confirm, adjust if needed, and start preparing.

  4. Live updates

    Customers get real‑time status updates—confirmed, preparing, ready, or out for delivery.

  5. Pickup or delivery

    Customers pick up in‑store or receive delivery as per your settings.
